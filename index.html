The Origin Story

Why PolicyWorth exists

When Senior Friendship Centers hired an in-house economist to prove the economic impact of its services, the obvious question was how a nonprofit could afford it. The truth: most can’t.

That’s why Darwin Tepe (economist) and Brandon Sisneros (financial analyst and former board member) built PolicyWorth—to make the same analysis accessible to every provider.
