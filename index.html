The Origin Story

Why PolicyWorth exists

When Senior Friendship Centers hired an in‑house economist to prove the economic impact of its services, the obvious question was how a nonprofit could afford it. The truth: most can’t.

That’s why Darwin Tepe (economist) and Brandon Sisneros (financial analyst and former board member) built PolicyWorth—to make the same analysis accessible to every provider.

What it does

Economist‑grade reports, instantly

Input a few key data points and generate the clear, persuasive reports used to secure millions from legislators and donors.

  • No spreadsheets
  • Built‑in benchmarks & assumptions
  • Ready to export
What you get

Training, sources, and strategy

Members get step‑by‑step training videos, a curated source library of academic & government data, and a members‑only blog with fresh HCBS strategies.

Why it matters

Prove value. Grow funding.

HCBS isn’t just better for seniors—it’s fiscally responsible for communities. For ~20% of the cost of hiring an economist, your team can prove it with confidence.
