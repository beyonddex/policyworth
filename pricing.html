Frequently asked questions

Why is annual access required?

Outdated impact information undermines board and donor trust. Annual access ensures all data, assumptions, and calculations remain accurate and appropriate for board-level use.

What's included in the initial engagement?

The initial engagement builds your custom economic impact model, 5–10 year funding forecast, and personalized dashboard. This ensures your data and projections are accurate and defensible.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes! You can upgrade or adjust your tier at any time. We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition and that you're getting the insights your organization needs.
