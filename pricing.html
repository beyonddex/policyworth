Frequently Asked Questions

Why is annual access required?
Outdated impact information undermines board and donor trust. Annual access ensures that all data, assumptions, and impact calculations remain accurate, current, and appropriate for board-level and donor-facing use.
What's included in the initial engagement?
The initial engagement builds your custom economic impact model, 5–10 year funding forecast, and personalized dashboard. This foundational work ensures your data, assumptions, and projections are accurate and defensible.
Can I switch tiers later?
Yes! You can upgrade or adjust your tier at any time. We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition and that you're getting the insights and support your organization needs.
